Raza Noodles

Noodles Done Right since 2012

We take noodles seriously!

Established in 2012 by two Thai brothers, we've been delivering top quality Asian Cousine.

We have two top-of-the-line ramen noodle machines. All of our noodles are freshly prepared in the restaurant's kitchen. Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish that we are proud to say ours is the best in Ireland. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth with toppings including sliced pork, dried seaweed, menma, and green onions, and is sometimes flavored with soy sauce or miso. From Kyushu's tonkotsu (pork bone broth) ramen to Hokkaido's miso ramen, nearly every area in Japan has its own version of ramen. We have a wide range of options in our kitchen.
